AWS announced availability of Spring 2026 SOC 1 and 2 compliance reports in machine-readable OSCAL (Open Security Controls Assessment Language) format alongside traditional PDF versions, covering 188 AWS services over a 12-month assessment period. AWS describes this as the first major cloud provider offering such reports in NIST's standardized format, enabling customers to automate compliance workflows and reduce manual processing.
